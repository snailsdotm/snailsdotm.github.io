Bodies

Opossums have gray fur and long bare tails. They carry babies on their back or in their pouch. They have sharp teeth. They don't carry rabies.

Diet

They are scavengers. They eat bugs, plants, berries, and dead animals. They will eat other dead opossums.

Habitat

They live in forests, near fences, and in backyards. They are moving into Minnesota due to climate change.
